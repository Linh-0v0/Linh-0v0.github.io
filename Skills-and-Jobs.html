Skills and Jobs

Monetization of Particeps require members of Sketchers showcase skillsets and roles beyond the scope of the IIT course of RMIT. Here what's an ideal team would look like...

Nguyen

Project manager and Quality Assurance Engineer
Assignment's Role

Particeps is the brainchild project of Nguyen H. Vu, IT major of RMIT. The Sketchers voted Particeps out of all 4 projects of each members to be the group's project for assignment 3. As the founder of the project, Nguyen knows the scopes and limits as well as the technologies behind Particeps. Therefore, it is only logical that Nguyen be the project manager of Particeps as well as the communicator between meetings.

Extended Role

Should Particeps be monetized, Nguyen's role as the flagship of the project will remain. He will oversee the individual departments that improve and maintain Particeps. Additionally, Nguyen will be the Quality Assurance Engineer, whose job is to make sure the product is up-to-date and commercial ready in terms of quality.

Sourabi

UI/UX designer
Assignment's Role

A Design major, Sourabi's talents speak when she designed Particeps' logo. Sourabi's skillsets like in good aethestics and user interface so she is tasked with making sure our websites and product are easy on the eyes. 

Extended Role

Should Particeps be monetized, Sourabi's role as UI designer will remain and augmented. SHe will be in charge of updating the interfaces of the templates (front-end development) and the product's official website.

Linh

Back-end Developer
Assignment's Role

Linh has a way with codes... She is in charge of creating the Particeps' sign-up and log-in page using Heroku as a version control online host for the page and MySQL as the programming language to store users' data.

Extended Role

Should Particeps' be monetized, Linh's job will remain more or less the same, only more advanced in degrees. She will now be responsible for more templates, upkeep of the websites and adding new features if requried. Linh's skillsets lie with programming languages, being an IT major and an aspirant in web development specialization.

Thao

Business Analyst and Marketing Director
Assignment's Role

Thao is in charge of writing parts of the report as well as preparing the PowerPoint slides for the
presentation and demo. Thao is a major in Digital Marketing so her portfolio includes presentation skills and status report writing.

Expanded Role

Should Particeps be monetized, Thao will be the Business Analyst and Marketing Director, whose final decision on business and financial moves of the Sketchers' Particeps will determine if this product turns a profit.

How do we communicate and progress our project?

Online Meetings

When our schedules conflict but we need updates or risks arise, we simply use Google Teams meet online

Offline Meetings

We schedule one or two meetings weekly to keep track of our progress. Offline meetings usually involve heavy coding or big subjects

Meeting minutes

We keep meeting minutes in form of Word documents for both online and offline meetings to know the agenda of the meeting and keep track of progress
