Overview

"Particeps"- Latin for "companions", like its namesake, accompanies and assists users in learning new ideas and absorbing new information. Its logo consists of a pencil, a protractor and a graduation hat in keeping with the theme of education. The bright red and blue colors, besides creating contrast for better display, incite a sense of excitement. Users create an account with Particeps, answer a survey and let the survey lead them to an approriate template depending on the outcome of said survey.

Project Description

Original Particeps' mechanisms

Due to time constraint and the ambitious scale of Particeps, for the purpose of presenting a tangible product in time for the assignment 3, we reduce and adjust the range of subjects (only English) and number of interfaces (only 3), as well as the technologies behind Particeps.

Particeps' final mechanism

You start from here

Sign-up and Login page

Once you have signed up, log in and it should leads you to Particeps' survey page

Once you finish the survey, the result say one of the following: "You are a visual/ text/ hybrid learner !!!" and will prompt you to click to the corresponding template. You can also choose the templates on the left on your own.

The text template has... well... texts! Lots of 'em!

The video Template has... well... videos! Lots of 'em!

The hybrid template is a mixture of texts, videos and photos, for those who want the best of both worlds!
