RMIT UNIVERSITY

It's been a journey!

Particeps' is the first group project of the Sketchers. Naturally, anything first has its ups and downs and hiccups. Particeps has taught our team great lessons and is the progenitor of many great upcoming projects. Though our IIT course is coming to an end, our Particeps' development is not! Stay tune to our website and Particeps' to see what's new!

Thank you for visiting The Sketchers' Webpage! scroll down to see our group reflection looking back to our journey working on Particeps!

A collective review

As a team, after more than 8 weeks getting to know and working alongside each other, each Sketchers all concur that we were not used to each others' styles at First, but gradually got to know each other and how we work during our project. Admittedly, the Final Product is reduced in scaled but it is a tangible product, a competitive edge compared with other groups in our course, thanks to our the sketchers' teamwork and ingenuity.
