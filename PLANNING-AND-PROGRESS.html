Why plan and track your project?

  Any project, especially big projects, requires teams to break them down into components, assign deadlines to each component and keep track of progress.

Meeting minutes

Document your progress! (and risks!)

Our team uses meeting minutes to document our meetings' agenda and compares them with the Gantt Chart Timetable to see if we are on track. Meeting minutes are also helpful in discovering hiccups!
