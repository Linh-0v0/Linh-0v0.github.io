Personality Test


I already know myself as an omnivert, so I have no problem cooperate with other people. Fortunately, every team members in my group are extremely friendly and cooperative. The group members always take turn in reminding the work in class. Anytime someone doesn’t understand something (personal/group assignment or anything) and then asks on the group chat, we always share the answer and knowledge. I find it very reassuring having these good teammates. It is now like we are actually being good friends and not simply feeling like being coworkers at the first time we knew each other.


Even though I’m inclined toward rationality, there are surprisingly lots of times I do things based on feeling. However, thanks to the teammates I have, despite the fact that I occasionally don’t feel like working, the teammates hold me accountable and help me keep up to date with the current assignment/work thanks to their enthusiasm and humour.

Being open-minded means listening to other people’s opinion, being cooperative makes it easier to partner with people and work on something. I think that as long as we have these traits, we can accept each other’s flaws and opinions while working on the group projects.
